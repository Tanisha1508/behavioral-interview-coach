MY STORY BANK (prep doc)

Story 1: The checkout rescue
FreshCart checkout redesign. Cart abandonment stuck at 31% for three quarters.
I ran funnel analysis nights for two weeks, found the address form was killing
mobile users, fought design for a one-page flow, shipped in 6 weeks.
Abandonment fell to 22%. Use for: data-driven decisions, conviction, execution.

Story 2: Killing my own project
The loyalty-points program was my own proposal from 2024. A/B showed negative
LTV at 90 days. I recommended killing it in the same review where founders
expected a scale-up plan, and moved the 6 engineers to fulfillment.
Use for: intellectual honesty, prioritization, failure.

Story 3: The triage-bot standoff
Six weeks of clinical ops refusing the triage bot at TeleWell. I sat in the
nurses' station for two days, found the real fear was liability not workflow,
got legal to draft an escalation protocol, and scoped v1 to symptoms only.
Use for: conflict, stakeholder management, empathy.
